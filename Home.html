GOALS AND OBJECTIVES ABOUT ULK BRIEFLY

Kigali independent university ULK is a private institution of higher learning founded on 15thMarch, 1996 and governed now by the nwe Law No27/2013
of 24/5/2013 governing the organisation and functioning of higher education in Rwanda, the Law No13/2009 of 27/5/2009 regulating labour in rwanda
the presidentual order No51/01 of 13/7/2010 establishing quality standards in higher learning insititutions, the ULK statutes, the presnt internal
Regulation and manual of procedures

The Philosophy of Kigali Independent university ULK is based on four fundamental principles:

    To have faith in God
  • To know one's mission on earth
  • To live ethical values
  • To have positive thoughts

The values of Kigali independent university ULK are "integrity,Humility,Determination and Excellence".

The Motto of Kigali independent university ULK is Science and Conscience

The fundamental of ULK is to provide students with a trainibg which will enable them tobecome actors and organisers of complete
development of the nation

ULK has the vision to stand as university for excellence at the heart of Africa with highly motivated students and a highly Qualified
endowed with ethical values
